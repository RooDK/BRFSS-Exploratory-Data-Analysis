# BRFSS-Exploratory-Data-Analysis
In the context of the healthcare system, data analytics can track the health of populations and identify people at risk for chronic diseases. So, implementing data analytics in healthcare saves lives, but also money. The Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System (BRFSS) is a collaborative project between all of the states in the United States (US) and participating US territories and the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC). BRFSS data support in-progress and  accomplished by using BRFSS data. I would like to explore the impact of these factors on cancer survivors.
